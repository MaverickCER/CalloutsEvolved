These days, everyone from 3 years old to 70 years young are picking up video games! Our current "mic required" culture does not provide the same opportunities to everyone. People who are deaf, mute, young, female, etc. often get kicked from groups or heckled while completing end game content.

More Reliable:

No mic lag, talking over each other, feedback, echoing, or faulty connections

More Focused:

16 customizable callouts allow you to prepare for all end game activities

More Inclusive:

Visual Callouts and Text to Speech provide accessible notifications for all players.

More Synchronized:

Sessions will sync visial callouts immediately and play sounds right after eachother.

More Efficient:

Trigger callouts and timers with your keyboard so that you can set and forget until the exact moment you launch an attack

More Convenient:

Play without squeaky voices, heavy breathing, and background noise thanks to real time continous speech to text

After years of trying everything from discord bots to soundboards, Callouts Evolved has grown into a capable, compatible, and inclusive web application helping 1000s of gamers communicate worldwide!
